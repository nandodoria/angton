Family Owned & Operated

Angton has served the electrical needs of London and surrounding area for over 25 years, offering professional electrical installation and repairs. We offer a full range of electrical services, as well as exceptional customer care and attention to detail.

Our Promise

To accomplish our mission, we respect the values of our customers by understanding the needs they require. We are locally and independently operated. Established in 1993, Angton Electric Ltd. is fast becoming a name for service excellence, integrity and reliability.
